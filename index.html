About the Artist

With over seven years of experience in the art of henna, I bring a refined blend of tradition, precision, and modern elegance to every design. Having built my artistry over four years in New Jersey, USA, I am now based in Ajax, Ontario, offering bespoke henna experiences for brides and special occasions.

My signature style lies in intricate Indian figure-element henna and finely detailed architectural patterns, thoughtfully balanced with soft. minimalistic designs that highlight delicate precision.

Known for exceptional attention to detail and commitment to perfection, I approach every design with patience and artistry. While my process is intentionally meticulous, the results are deeply intricate, graceful, and long-lasting stain--crafted to leave a lasting impression.

Policies

Booking Terms & Client Guidelines

1. Booking & Deposit

  • A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking
  • Dates are confirmed only after deposit is received
  • Remaining balance must be paid on or before appointment day

2. Rescheduling

  • Requests must be made at least 48 hours in advance
  • New dates are subject to availability
  • Deposits are non-transferable for last-minute changes

3. Cancellations

  • Deposits are strictly non-refundable within 30 days
  • Cancellations within 14 days may be subject to full charge

4. Travel

  • Travel fees apply based on location
  • Parking arrangements must be provided by the client

5. Event Bookings (Guest Henna)

  • Minimum booking duration may apply for distant locations
  • Design flow adjusted to accommodate all guests
  • Guests must be ready during scheduled time

6. Preparations

  • Hands/skin must be clean and free from oils, lotions, or water

7. Aftercare Responsibility

  • Proper aftercare is essential for dark stain results
